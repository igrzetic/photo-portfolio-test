Amelia Allen is a lifestyle, portrait and documentary photographer from Somerset who now lives and works in London.

ABOUT ME

Starting her career in fashion photography at the age of 18, it was on the sets of high end brand campaigns and fashion shows that Amelia learned the technicalities of what has become her signature style. Having always been drawn to sociological documentary photography, Amelia applies her classic and energetic style seamlessly to her portraiture and documentary projects.
